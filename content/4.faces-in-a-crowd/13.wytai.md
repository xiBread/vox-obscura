::definition
*n.* a feature of modern civilization that suddenly strikes you as absurd and grotesque&mdash;from
pets and milk drinking to organ transplants, life insurance, and fiction&mdash;part of a rich legacy
of absurdity that dates all the way back to the moment our ancestors first hauled themselves out of
the slime, but could not for the life of them remember what they got up to do.

#details
Acronym of When You Think About it. Pronounced "*wahy*-tahy."
::
