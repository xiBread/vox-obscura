#definition
*n.* the sense that you're just making it up as you go along&mdash;knowing that if someone asked why
you do most things, you couldn't really come up with a convincing explanation.

#details
In Ancient Rome, *Ludi Osci*, the Oscan Games, gave rise to the art of improvisational theater.
Pronounced "loo-dee-*oh*-sis."
