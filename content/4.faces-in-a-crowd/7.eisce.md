::definition
*n.* the awareness of the infinitesimal role you play in shaping your own society&mdash;knowing that
whenever you smile at a stranger, pronounce a word a certain way, laugh at a certain joke, or choose
the slightly shinier apple, you are unwittingly helping to construct the world in which you
live&mdash;a role both vanishingly small but also somehow daunting, making it that much harder to
complain about the traffic, knowing that you are traffic.

#details
Irish *eisceacht*, exception. Pronounced "*ahy*-shuh."
::
