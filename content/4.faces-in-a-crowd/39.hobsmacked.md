::definition
*adj.* suddenly aware of how limited your social circles really are; that although your immediate
environment feels like a microcosm of society, it's more like a bag of exotic fish floating on the
surface of a huge aquarium, which is teeming with a million shadowy subcultures that you'd be
stunned to see up close.

#details
From *hobnob*, to mix socially + *gobsmacked*, astounded.
::
