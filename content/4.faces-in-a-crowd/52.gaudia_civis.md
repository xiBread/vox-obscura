::definition
*n.* a humble pulse of gratification you feel when acting as a citizen&mdash;serving on a jury,
standing in line at a polling place, taking part in a debate at a town meeting&mdash;where you can
actually feel the gears of democracy turning ever so slightly, because you actually had a hand in
it.

#details
Latin *gaudia*, joys + *civis*, citizen. Pronounced "*gou*-dee-uh *siv*-is".
::
