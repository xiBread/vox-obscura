#definition
*n.* the meditative atmosphere of being alone in a bathroom, sequested inside your own little
isolation booth, enjoying a moment backstage from the razzle-dazzle of public life.

#details
Latin *pax*, a period of peace + *latrina*, toilet. Compare *Pax Romana* or *Pax Americana*;
sometimes the solace of bathroom stalls can feel just as profound as the protection of empires.
Pronounced "paks luh-*tree*-nah."
