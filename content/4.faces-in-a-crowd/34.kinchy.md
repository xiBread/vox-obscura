#definition
*adj.* feeling guilty that you care about your own petty concerns more deeply than faraway
cataclysms&mdash;that a family spat hurts more than a civil war, that a three-day fever hits you
harder than climate change.

#details
Japanese 近視 (*kinshi*), near-sightedness. A word just shy of *kinship*. Pronounced "*kin*-chee."
