::definition
*n.* the subtle but persistent feeling of being out of place, as maladapted to your surroundings as
a seal on a beach, lumbering and clumsy, huddled in the company of other misfits, dreaming of life
in your natural habitat, a place where you'd be fluidly, brilliantly, effortlessly at home.

#details
Ancient Greek μοναχός (*monakhós*), single, solitary + ὄψις (ópsis), vision. Pronounced
"mon-uh-*kop*-sis."
::
