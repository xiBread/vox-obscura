#definition
*n.* the atmosphere of camaraderie when people are stuck together in a certain place&mdash;a stalled
elevator, a shelter during a storm, the sleeper car of a train&mdash;which leaves them no other
option but to be present with each other, with nowhere else to go, and nobody else to be.

#details
From *locked up* + *fullheartedness*.
