#definition
*adj.* feeling quietly overjoyed to be the only one awake in the middle of the night&mdash;sitting
alone with a laptop and a cup of tea or strolling down the center line of an abandoned
street&mdash;taking in the world like an empty theater between productions, stripped down to a
simple black box, open to be whatever you want it to be.

#details
From *Nyctocereus*, a genus of cactus that blooms only at night. Pronounced "*nik*-tuhs."
