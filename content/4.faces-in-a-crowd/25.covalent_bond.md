#definition
*n.* a moment of sudden involvement in a stranger's personal life&mdash;rushing over to break up a
fight, helping a teary-eyed parent struggling with a stroller, righting someone's bike after a bad
fall&mdash;that shatters the invisible glass box that usually surrounds us in public, the one we
prefer to pretend is impenetrable, which somehow renders us unable to speak.

#details
From *covalence*, literally "shared strength." In chemistry, a *covalent bond* describes the force
that holds two atoms together upon sharing a loose outer shell of electron pairs. Pronounced
"ko-*vey*-luhnt bond."
