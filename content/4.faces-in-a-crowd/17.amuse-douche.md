#definition
*n.* an activity that you've adored since you were a kid&mdash;riding bikes, reading books, taking
pictures, cooking food&mdash;whose enjoyment dissolves on contact with hardcore fanatics' ferocious
obsession with technique.

#details
From *amuse-bouche*, a bite-size appetizer intended to tantalize the palate + *douche*. Pronounced
"ah-mooz-*doosh*."
