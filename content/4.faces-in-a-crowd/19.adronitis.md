::definition
*n.* frustration with how long it takes to get to know someone&mdash;spending the first few weeks
chatting in their psychological entryway, with each subsequent conversation like entering a
different anteroom, each a little closer to the center of the home&mdash;wishing instead that you
could start there and work your way out, exchanging your deepest secrets first, before easing into
casualness, until you've built up enough mystery over the years to ask them where they're from and
what they do for a living.

#details
In Ancient Roman architecture, an *andronitis* is a hallway connecting the front part of the house
with a complex inner atrium. One quirk of Roman houses is that all the rooms in the front have Greek
names, but all the back rooms are in Latin&mdash;as if your outer self and your inner self are
speaking in completely different languages. Pronounced "ad-roh-*nahy*-tis."
::
