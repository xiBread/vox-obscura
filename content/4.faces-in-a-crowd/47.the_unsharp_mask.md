::definition
*n.* the tendency for special networks to magnify unexpected personality traits of people you know,
a consequence of compressing someone's raw persona into a low-res digital format, which can randomly
brighten their outer glow, sharpen their comic edges, darken their shadows, or add motion blur to a
stationary life.

#details
In digital photo processing, *unsharp mask*, is a filter that combines the original negative with a
blurred positive image. The resulting image may look sharper but is actually a less accurate
interpretation of the subject.
::
