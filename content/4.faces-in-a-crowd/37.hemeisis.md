::definition
*n.* the off-putting awareness of how deeply your culture's norms are ingrained in your
psyche&mdash;arbitrarily defining what you find shameful and admirable, private and communal,
attractive and repulsive, fair and unfair&mdash;as if you'd been programmed in a way that you can't
control or even perceive, until you happen to encounter someone who has slightly different code.

#details
Ancient Greek ἡμεῖς (*hēmeîs*), we (excluding the listener). Pronounced "hem-*ey*-sis."
::
