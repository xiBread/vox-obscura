#definition
*n.* a state of instinctive restlessness that arises when your society makes too many choices on
your behalf&mdash;foreclosing all risks, codifying all moral dilemmas, deciding in advance whether
you'll suceed or fail&mdash;as if your conscience had been outsourced to an external provider, so
it's no longer neccessary to have one on site.
