::definition
*n.* a state of exhaustion with how cruel people can be, freely undercutting each other in ways that
seem petty and gratuitous&mdash;which can sometimes trigger a countervailing sense of gratitude for
things that are kind, sincere, forgiving, or unabashedly joyful.

#details
Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (*ánthrōpos*), humanity + ὀδύνη (*odúnē*), sorrow, anguish, pain. Pronounced
"an-thruh-*din*-ee-uh."
::
