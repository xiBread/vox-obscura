#definition
*n.* the frustration of knowing how neatly you embody a certain stereotype, without even intending
to; the sense of bafflement at the extent to which we all seem to hew to certain tropes, each
looking pretty much how you'd expect them to look, as if we're all trick-or-treating through society
wearing one of a handful of premade costumes, because we're tired of answering the question, "What
are you supposed to be?"

#details
Ancient Greek μῖμος (*mîmos*), imitator, actor + μῖσος (*mîsos*), hatred. Pronounced
"mim-ee-*oh*-mee-uh."
