::definition
*n.* the fear of speaking off the cuff or from the heart; the terror of saying the wrong thing and
having to watch someone's smile fade as they realize you're not who they thought you were.

#details
Ancient Greek μῶμος (*momos*), blemish, disgrace + -φοβία (-*phobia*), fear. Momus was the Ancient
Greek god of mockery and harsh criticism. Pronounced "moh-muh-*foh*-bee-uh."
::
