::definition
*n.* a place that seems to hold profound significance to everyone else but you&mdash;the sacred
temple of some other faith, a random fence post festooned with flowers, a crowd cheering for a team
you've never heard of&mdash;which leaves you trying to coax yourself into feeling something anyway,
like inserting your house key into a random lock just to feel if something clicks.

#details
From *holy*, sacred or religiously revered + *-ette*, denoting an imitation of the real thing.
Pronounced "hoh-lee-*et*."
::
