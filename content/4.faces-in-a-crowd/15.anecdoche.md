::definition
*n.* a conversation in which everyone is talking but nobody is listening&mdash;instead merely
overlaying words like a spoken game of Scrabble, each player borrowing bits of others' anecdotes to
build out their own, until we reach a point when we all run out of things to say.

#details
From *anecdote*, a short and often amusing account of real-life events + *synecdoche*, a figure of
speech in which a part stands for the whole. Pronounced "un-*nek*-doh-kee."
::
