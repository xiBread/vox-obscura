#definition
*n.* the complicated solitude of hotel rooms late at night, spending time in a place that's both
yours and emphatically not yours, both soulless and homey, both timeless and temporary, suspended
somewhere halfway between vacancy and no vacancy.

#details
From *inn*, a small hotel or tavern for travelers + *inanity*, a total lack of meaning or ideas.
Pronounced "*ihn*-i-tee."
