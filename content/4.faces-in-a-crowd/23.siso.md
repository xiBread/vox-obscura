::definition
*n.* a solitary experience you wish you could have shared with someone else&mdash;having dinner in a
romantic setting, reaching the summit after an arduous climb, having a run-in with a crazy stranger
that nobody's going to believe&mdash;which makes you look around for confirmation that it even
happened at all.

#details
Welsh *si-so*, see-saw, an invention that can only ever be enjoyed by multiple people; when used
alone, it's just a wonky bench. Pronounced "*see*-soh."
::
