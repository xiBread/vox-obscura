#definition
*n.* a mysterious aura of loneliness you feel in certain places; the palpable weight of all the
lonely people secretly holed up in their houses and apartments, with a flickering blue glow cast up
on their walls&mdash;so many of whom might just want someone to talk to, or just want to feel
needed, and could be that for each other if they only could somehow connect.

#details
Short for "All the lonely people," from the song "Eleanor Rigby" by the Beatles. Pronounced
"*al*-uh-pee."
