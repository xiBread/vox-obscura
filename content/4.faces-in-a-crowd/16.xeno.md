#definition
*n.* the smallest measurable unit of human connection, typically exchanged between passing
strangers&mdash;a warm smile, a sympathetic nod, a shared laugh about some old
coincidence&mdash;moments that are fleeting and random but still contain powerful emotional
nutrients that can alleviate the symptoms of feeling alone.

#details
Ancient Greek ξένος (*xénos*), alien, stranger. Pronounced "*zee*-noh."
