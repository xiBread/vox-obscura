::definition
*adj.* humbled by how readily you place your life into the hands of random strangers, often without
a second thought&mdash;trusting a restaurant to check its expiration dates, trusting a construction
crew not to cheap out on the materials, trusting thousands of other drivers to stay in their
lane&mdash;people who you may never meet but whose well-being you're deeply invested in, whether you
know it or not.

#details
Danish *tillid*, trust. Pronounced "*til*-id."
::
