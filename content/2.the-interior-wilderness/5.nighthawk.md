::definition
*n.* a recurring though that only seems to strike you late at night&mdash;an overdue task, a nagging
guilt, a looming future&mdash;which you sometimes manage to forget for weeks, only to feel it land
on your shoulder once again, quietly building a nest.

#details
*Nighthawks* is a famous painting by Edward Hopper, depicting a lonely corner dinner late at night.
In logging, a *nighthawk*, is a metal ball that slid up and down a riverboat's flagpole, to aid
pilots in navigation.
::
