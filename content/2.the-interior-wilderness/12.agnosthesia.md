#definition
*n.* the state of not knowing how you really feel about something, which forces you to sift through
clues hidden in your own behavior, as if you were some other person&mdash;noticing a twist of acid
in your voice, an obscene amount of effort you put into something trifling, or an inexplicable
weight on your shoulders that makes it difficult to get out of bed.

#details
Ancient Greek ἄγνωστος (*àgnōstos*), not knowing + διάθεσις (*diáthesis*), condition, mood.
Pronounced "ag-nos-*thee*-zhuh."
