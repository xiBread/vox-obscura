#definition
*n.* the wish that you could see statistics overlaid on every person you encounter&mdash;checking
the signal strength of their compatibility, a measure of their trustworthiness, or even just an
emoji that tells you how they're really feeling.

#details
From the two symbols of binary code, *1* and *0*, denoting "on" or "off" + *I am*. Pronounced
"ahy-*yo*-yuh."
