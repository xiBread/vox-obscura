#definition
*n.* an abrupt collapse of trust in yourself&mdash;having abandoned a resolution, surrendered to
your demons, or squandered an opportunity you swore you'd take seriously this time&mdash;which
resets your expectations and makes it that much harder to guarantee that your word is worth
anything, even to yourself.

#details
In economics, a *loss of backing* is when the government no longer guarantees the value of a certain
currency, particularly when it's not exchangeable for anything physical like gold or silver, thus it
only retains value because we say it does.
