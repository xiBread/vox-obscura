::definition
*n.* an excuse you come up with to rationalize a disappointing outcome&mdash;telling yourself you
weren't in the mood for that sold-out show anyway, that your safety school is actually a better fit,
that your dream job might have been a bit too stressful.

#details
From the phrase, "Well, I...um...," which typically precedes such an excuse. Pronounced
"*wel*-ee-uhm."
::
