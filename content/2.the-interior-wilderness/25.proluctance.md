::definition
*n.* the paradoxical urge to avoid doing something you've been looking forward to&mdash;opening a
decisive letter, meeting up with a friend who's finally back in town, reading a new book from your
favorite author&mdash;perpetually waiting around for the right state of mind, stretching out the
bliss of anticipation as long as you can.

#details
Latin *pro-*, forward + *reluctans*, resisting. Pronounced "proh-*luhk*-tuhns."
::
