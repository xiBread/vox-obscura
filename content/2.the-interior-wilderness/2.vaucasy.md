#definition
*n.* the fear that you're little more than a product of your circumstances, that for all the thought
you put into shaping your beliefs and behaviors and relationships, you're essentially a dog being
trained by whatever stimuli you happen to encounter&mdash;reflexively drawn to whoever gives you
reliable hits of pleasure, skeptical of ideas that make you feel powerless.

#details
From Jacques de Vaucanson, a French engineer who built a series of lifelike programmable machines
(automata), including his masterpiece the *Digesting Duck*, which could flap its wings and quack,
drink water, and digest bits of grain into stimulated excrement. Pronounced "*vaw*-kuh-see."
