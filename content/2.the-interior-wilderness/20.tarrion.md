#definition
*n.* an odd interval of blankness you feel after something big happens to you
but before you feel the resulting emotional reaction&mdash;stunned by a sudden
loss, a stroke of luck, or an unexpected visitor&mdash;like those tension-filled
seconds between a flash of lightning and the thunderclap that follows, which
gives you a hint of how near you are to the coming storm.

#details
From *tarry*, to be late to react, or linger in expectation + *carry on*.
Pronounced "*tar*-ee-uhn."
