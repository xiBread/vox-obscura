#definition
*n.* the moment you realize you are changing as a person, finally outgrowing your old problems like
a reptile shedding its skin, already able to twist back around and chuckle at this weirdly
antiquated caricature of yourself that will soon come off completely.

#details
From *apolysis*, the stage of molting when an invertebrate's shell begins to separate from the skin
beneath it + *adultus*, sacrificed. Pronounced "ah-*pahl*-i-tuhs."
