#definition
*n.* the ambient feeling of being a human being; a baseline mood that everyone
feels intensely every moment of their lives, but can never pin down because they
have nothing else to compare it to.

#details
Sanskrit *manusyá*, human being. Pronounced "muh-*noo*-zhuh" or
"muh-*noo*-zee-uh."
