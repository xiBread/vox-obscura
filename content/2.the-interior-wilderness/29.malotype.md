#definition
*n.* a certain person who embodies all the things you like the least about
yourself&mdash;a seeming caricature of your worst tendencies&mdash;which leaves
you feeling repulsed and fascinated in equal measure, having stumbled upon a
role model of exactly the kind of person you never want to be.

#details
Latin *malus*, bad + *typus*, a kind of sculptor's mold. A mold is essentially a
negative image of the object you want to sculpt&mdash;so if you're trying to
shape yourself, perhaps a good first step is to scoop away the negative space.
Pronounced "*mal*-uh-tahyp."
