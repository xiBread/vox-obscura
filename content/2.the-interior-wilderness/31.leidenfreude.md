::definition
*n.* a sense of paradoxical relief when something bad happens to you, which temporarily lowers your
own expectations for yourself, transforming a faceless protagonist into something of an underdog,
who's that much easier to root for.

#details
German *Leiden*, suffering + *Freude*, joy. Compare *Schadenfreude*, joy at the misfortune of
others. Pronounced "*lahyd-n-froi-duh."
::
