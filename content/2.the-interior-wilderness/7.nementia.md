#definition
*n.* the post-distraction effor to recall the reason you're feeling particularly anxious or angry or
excited, trying to retrace your sequence of thoughts like a kid gathering the string of a downed
kite.

#details
Ancient Greek νέμειν (*némein*), to give what is due + Latin *dementia*, without mind. Pronounced
"ne-*men*-shuh."
