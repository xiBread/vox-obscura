#definition
*adj.* unable to settle the question of whether you're doing okay in life; feeling torn between
conflicting value systems and moveable goalposts, which makes you long for someone to come along and
score your progress in discrete and measurable units&mdash;points, dollars, friends, followers, or a
grade point average&mdash;which may not clear up where you're going but would at least reassure you
that you're one step closer to getting there.

#details
From *addled*, muddled or unclear + *worth*.
