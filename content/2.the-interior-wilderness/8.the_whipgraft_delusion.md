---

::definition
the whipgraft delusion

#definition
*n.* the phenomenon in which you catch your reflection in the mirror and get the
sense that you're peering into the eyes of a stranger, as if you're looking at a
police sketch of your own face aged forward twenty years, which would imply that
the real you is out there somewhere, wandering the streets of your old
neighborhood, still at large.

#details
In horticulture, *whip grafting* is when you fuse the top of one plant to the
bottom of another. In psychology, *the Capgras delusion* is the conviction that
a loved one has been replaced by an identical-looking imposter.
::
