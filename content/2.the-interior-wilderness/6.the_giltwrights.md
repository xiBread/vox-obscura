::definition
*n.* the imaginary committee of elders that keeps a running log of all your mistakes, steadily
building their case that you're secretly a fraud, a coward, a doofus, and a douche&mdash;who
would've revoked your good fortune years ago had they not been hampered by their own bitter
squabblings over proper grammer and spelling.

#details
Old English *gilt*, awareness of wrongdoing + *wrought*, shaped with hammers. Pronounced
"the *gilt*-rahyts."
::
