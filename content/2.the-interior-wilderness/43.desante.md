#definition
*n.* the brooding delirium of being sick, which makes time slow to a trickle and turns even the most
pathetic of tasks into monumental struggles, until the act of lifting your head from the pillow
feels like trying to climb a mountain, wondering if you'll ever find your way back again, or even
catch your breath.

#details
A riff on the French toast *santé*. Literally, "un-health!" Pronounced "day-sahn-*tey*."
::
