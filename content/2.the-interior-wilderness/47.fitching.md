::definition
*v. intr.* compulsively turning away from works of art you find frustratingly, nauseatingly
good&mdash;wanting to shut off the film and leave the theater, or devour a book only in maddening
little chunks&mdash;because it resonates at precisely the right frequency to rattle you to your
core, which makes it mildly uncomfortable to be yourself.

#details
From *bitching*, markedly good + *fitch*, the European polecat, an animal that often cripples its
prey by piercing its brain with its teeth, before storing it alive in its burrow to return and eat
sometime later.
::
