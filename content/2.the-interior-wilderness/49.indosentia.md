#definition
*n.* the fear that your emotions might feel profound but are crudely biological, less to do with
meaning and philosophy than with hormones, endorphins, sleep cycles, and blood sugar&mdash;any of
which might easily be tweaked to induce unfalsifiable feelings of joy, depression, bloodlust, or
kinship, or even a spiritual transcendence of your physical body.

#details
Acronym of the supposed "happy chemicals" Dopamine, Oxytocin, Serotonin, and Endorphins + *in*
*absentia*, something done in your absence. Pronounced "in-duh-*sen*-shah" or "in-doh-*sen*-tyah."
