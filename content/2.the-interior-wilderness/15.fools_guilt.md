#definition
*n.* a pulse of shame you feel even though you've done nothing
wrong&mdash;passing a police car while under the speed limit, being carded after
legally ordering a drink, or exiting a store without buying anything.

#details
From *fool's gold* + *guilt*. Also known as a *reverse Alford plea*, whereby you
plead innocent to all charges but want the judge to know that you feel kinda
guilty anyway.
