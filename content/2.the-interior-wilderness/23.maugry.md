#definition
*adj.* afraid that you've become mentally deranged all your life and everybody
around you knows, but none of them mention it to your directly because they feel
it's not their place.

#details
From *maunder*, to mumble indistinctly + *maugre*, in spite of, notwithstanding.
Pronounced "*maw*-gree."
