#definition
*n.* the unsettling awareness of your own heartbeat, whose tenuous muscular
throbbing feels less like a metronome than nervous ditty your heart is tapping
to itself, as if to casually remind the outside world, *I'm here, I'm here, I'm*
*here*.

#details
In music, *tempo rubato*, "stolen time," is a slight speeding up and slowing
down of the tempo of a piece, borrowing time from one measure then paying it
back later. Pronounced "roo-bah-*toh*-sis."
