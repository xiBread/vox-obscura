::definition
heartspur

#definition
*n.* an unexpected surge of emotion in response to a seemingly innocuous
trigger&mdash;the distinctive squeal of a rusty fence, a key change in an old
pop song, the hint of a certain perfume&mdash;which feels all the more intense
because you can't quite pin it down.

#details
From *heart* + *spur*, a spike on a heel that urges a horse to move forward.
