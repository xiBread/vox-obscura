::definition
*n.* an important part of your personality that others seldom see&mdash;a secret flaw, a hidden
talent, trauma that never comes up, dreams you never mention&mdash;that remains a vital part of who
you are even if nobody knows it's there, like the sprawling archives in the attics of museums,
packed with works far too priceless to risk being displayed for the public.

#details
From a *keep*, the innermost tower of a castle.
::
