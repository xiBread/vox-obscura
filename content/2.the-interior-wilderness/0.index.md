# The Interior Wilderness

::quotation
---
author: D. H. Lawrence
source: Studies in Clssic American Literature
---

This is what I believe:\
["*That I am I.*"]{.indent-1}\
["*That my soul is a dark forest.*"]{.indent-1}\
["*That my known self will never be more than a little clearing*]{.indent-1}\
[*in the forest.*"]{.indent-2}\
["*That gods, strange gods, come forth from the forest into the*]{.indent-1}\
[*clearing of my known self, and then go back.*"]{.indent-2}\
["*That I must have the courage to let them come and go.*"]{.indent-1}\
["*That I will never let mankind put anything over me, but*]{.indent-1}\
[*that I will try always to recognize and submit to the gods*]{.indent-2}\
[*in me and the gods in other men and women.*"]{.indent-2}\
There is my creed.
::
