#definition
*n.* the dispiriting awareness that the twists and turns of your life feel new and profound but are
not unique&mdash;marked by the same coming-of-age struggles as millions of others, the same career
setbacks, the same family strife, the same learning curfe of parenting&mdash;which makes even your
toughest challenges feel harmless and predictable, just another remake of the same old story.

#details
A riff on *blues standards*, the catalog of the most popular songs in the blues genre, which is
itself famous for chord progressions that cycle through variations on a theme.
