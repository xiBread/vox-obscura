::definition
mahpiohanzia

#definition
*n*. the frustration of being unable to fly, unable to stretch out your arms and
vault into the air, having finally shrugged off the burden of your own weight,
which you've been carrying your entire life without a second thought.

#details
Lakota *mahpiohanzi*, "a shadow caused by a cloud." Pronounced
"mah-pee-oh-*han*-zee-uh."
