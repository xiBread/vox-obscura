::definition
*n.* the nagging sense that no matter how many days you spend exploring a foreign country, you never
quite manage to step foot in it&mdash;instead floating high above the culture like a diver over a
reef, too dazzled by its exotic quirks to notice its problems and complexities and banalities, while
drawing from the heavy tank of assumptions that you carry on your back wherever you go.

#details
From *vulture*, an animal that hovers high over its prey + *culture shock*, the confusion of having
to adjust to a culture different than the one you're used to.
::
