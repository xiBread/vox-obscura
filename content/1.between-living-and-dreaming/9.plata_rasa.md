::definition
plata rasa

#definition
*n*. the lulling sound of a running dishwasher, whose steady maternal shushing
somehow puts you completely at peace with not having circumnavigated anything
solo.

#details
Latin *plata*, plate + *rasa*, blank or scraped clean. Pronounced
"*pla*-tuh-*rah*-suh."
::
