::definition
*adj.* feeling grateful to be stranded in a place where you can't do much of anything&mdash;sitting
for hours at an airport gate, the sleeper car of a train, or the backseat of a van on a long road
trip&mdash;which temporarily alleviates the burden of being able to do anything at any time and
frees up your brain to do whatever it wants to do, even if it's just to flicket your eyes across the
passing landscape.

#details
From *Idlewild*, the original name of John F. Kennedy International Airport in New York City.
::
