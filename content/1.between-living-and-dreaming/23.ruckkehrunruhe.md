#definition
*n.* the feeling of returning from an immersive trip only to notice it fading
rapidly from your awarness, as if your brain had automatically assumed it was
all just a dream and already went to work scrubbing it from your memory.

#details
German *rückkehren*, coming back + *Unruhe*, restlessness. Compare *Zugunruhe*,
"migration restlessness," the fidgety behavior observed in birds approaching
mirgation. Pronounced "rook-*kair*-oon-roo-uh."
::
