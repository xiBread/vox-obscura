::definition
la cuna

#definition
*n.* a twinge of sadness that there's no frontier left that as the last explorer
trudged his armies toward the last blank spot on the map, he didn't suddenly
turn for home, leaving one last island unexplored so we could set it aside as a
strategic reserve of mystery.

#details
Latin *lacuna*, an unfilled space or hole + Spanish *la cuna*, cradle.
Pronounced "lah *koo*-nuh."
::
