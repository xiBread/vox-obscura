#definition
*n.* the moment you wake up from an immersive dream and have to abruptly
recalibrate to the real world&mdash;unquitting your job, falling right back out
of love, reburying your lost loved ones.

#details
In American football, the *drop kick* is when a player drops the ball and kicks
it as it bounces off the ground, used as a method of restarting play.
