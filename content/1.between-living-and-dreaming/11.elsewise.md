::definition
elsewise

#definition
*adj*. struck by the poignant strangeness of other people's homes, which smell
and feel so different than your own&mdash;seeing the details of their private
living space, noticing their little daily rituals, the way they've arranged
their things, the framed photos of people you'll never know.

#details
From *else*, other + *wise*, with reference to.
