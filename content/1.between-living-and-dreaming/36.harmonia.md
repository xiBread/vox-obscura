#definition
*n.* an itchy sense of dread when life feels just a hint too peaceful&mdash;when everyone seems to
get along suspiciously well, with an eerie stillness that makes you want to brace for the inevitable
collapse, or burn it down yourself.

#details
From *harmony* + *paranoia*. Pronounced "hahr-muh-*noi*-uh."
