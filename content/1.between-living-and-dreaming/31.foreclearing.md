#definition
*n.* the act of deliberately refusing to learn the scientific explanations of
things out of fear that it'll ruin the magic&mdash;turning flower petals into
tacky billboards, decoding birdsong into trash talk, defracting a rainbow back
inside its tiny prism.

#details
Danish *forklaring*, explanation. Pronounced "fohr-*kleer*-ing."
