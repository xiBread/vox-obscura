#definition
*n.* a hypothetical conversation that you compulsively play out in your
head&mdash;a crisp analysis, a devastating comeback, a cathartic
heart-to-heart&mdash;which serves as a kind of psychological batting cage that
feels far more satisfying than the small-ball strategies of everyday life.

#details
French *jusqu'à*, until. In baseball, "small ball" is a cautious offensive
strategy devoted to getting on base via walks, bunts, and steals, forgoing the
big home run moments that fans tend to enjoy. Pronounced "*zhoos*-ka."
