#definition
*n.* the moment you look around and realize you're currently happy&mdash;consciously trying to savor
the feeling&mdash;which prompts your intellect to identify it, pick it apart, and put it in context,
where it will slowly dissolve until it's little more than an aftertaste.

#details
Ancient Greek καιρός (*kairos*), a sublime or opportune moment + σκλήρωσις (*sklḗrōsis*), hardening.
Pronounced "kahy-roh-skluh-*roh*-sis."
