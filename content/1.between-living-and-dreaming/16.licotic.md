::definition
licotic

#definition
*adj.* anxiously excited to introduce a friend to something you think is
amazing&mdash;a classic album, a favorite restaurant, a TV show they're lucky
enough to watch for the very first time&mdash;which prompts you to continually
poll their face waiting for the inevitable rush of awe, only to cringe when you
discover all the work's flaws shining through for the very first time.

#details
Old English *licode*, it pleased \[you] + *psychotic*. Pronounced
"lahy-*kot*-ic."
