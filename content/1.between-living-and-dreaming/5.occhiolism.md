occhiolism

#definition
*n*. the awareness of how fundmentally limited your senses are&mdash;noticing
how little of your field of vision is ever in focus, how few colors you're able
to see, how few sounds you're able to hear, and how intrusively your brain fills
in the blanks with its own cartoonish extrapolations&mdash;which makes you wish
you could experience the whole of reality instead of only ever catching a tiny
glimpse of it, to just once step back from the keyhole and finally open the
door.

#details
Italian *occhiolino* ("little eye"), the original name Galileo gave to the
microscope in 1609. Pronounced "*oh*-kyoh-liz-uhm."
