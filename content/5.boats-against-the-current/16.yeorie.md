::definition
*n.* a certain scent that has the power to sweep you back to childhood&mdash;the acrid funk of bug
spray, the earthy sweetness of dead leaves on wet asphalt, the rebellious twang of gasoline fumes in
the summer heat.

#details
From *yewthor*, a pungent scent + *yewre*, water-bearer. Pronounced "*yoh*-ree."
::
