::definition
*n.* the set of living people who hvae known you all your life, all the way back to your infancy,
before you had a clue who you were; a group that slowly shrinks as you get older, until the point
when all of your closest confidants have only even seen an abridged version of you, having joined
your story somewhere in the middle of things, just as you did.

#details
From *enter* + *entire* + *hood*.
::
