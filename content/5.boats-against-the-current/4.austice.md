::definition
*n.* a wistful omen of the first sign of autumn&mdash;a subtle coolness in the shadows, a rustling
of dead leaves abandoned on the sidewalk, or a long skein of geese sweeping over your head like the
second hand of a clock.

#details
From *autumn* + *auspice*, an omen, or a divination derived from observing the actions of birds.
Pronounced "*aw*-stis."
::
