::definition
*n.* the desire to hold on to time as it passes, like trying to keep your grip on a rock in the
middle of a river, feeling the weight of the current against your chest while your elders float on
downstream, calling over the roar of the rapids, "*Just let go&mdash;it's okay&mdash;let go.*"

#details
From *anchorage*, a port at which ships may anchor. Pronounced "*ang*-ker-ij."
::
