::definition
*n.* the feeling of emptiness after a long and arduous process if finally complete&mdash;having
finished school, recovered from surgery, or gone home at the end of your wedding&mdash;which leaves
you relieved that it's over but missing the stress that organized your life into a mission.

#details
Norwegian *etter*, after + *råtne*, decay. Pronounced "*et*-er-rath."
::
