::definition
*n.* an imaginary conversation with an old photo of yourself, in which you might offer them a word
of advice&mdash;to banish your worries, soak it all in, or shape up before it's too late&mdash;or
maybe just ask them if they thought you had done justice to the life they built for you.

#details
From *daguerreotype*, a form of early portrait photography + *dialogue*. Pronounced
"duh-*gair*-uh-lawg."
::
