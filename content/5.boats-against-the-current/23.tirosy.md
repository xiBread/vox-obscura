::definition
*n.* a complicated feeling of envy and admiration for people younger than you&mdash;their eyes
shining with energy, their futures rich with potential, their confidence smooth and untouched like a
freshly opened jar of peanut butter, which you simultaneously want to preserve forever and gleefully
undercut.

#details
Latin *tiros*, beginners, new recruits + *jealousy*. Pronounced "*teer*-uh-see."
::
