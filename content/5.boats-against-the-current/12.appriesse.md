::definition
*n.* the feeling of loss that you never had a chance to meet a certain person before they died,
which compels you to try to get to know them anyway, gathering snapshots and stories to build out a
sketch of who they were, learning them like a character in a novel, which makes them feel all the
more alive even though you've already skipped ahead and read the last page.

#details
Latin *appretiare*, to appraise + *ad pressum*, after. Pronounced "ap-ree-*es*."
::
