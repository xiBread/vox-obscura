::definition
*adj.* frustrated that you can't force yourself to remember something, even though it's right on the
tip of your tongue&mdash;wishing you could simple rifle through your own files directly, rather than
having to toss random scraps to your team of mental archivists, who evidently need hours to sift
through the pile before they come up with an answer, just as you're falling asleep.

#details
From *pither*, to dig lightly. Pronounced "*pith*-erd."
::
