::definition
*n.* a long-forgotten mistake from your past that could reappear at any time and rip your life
apart, like a boomerang you tossed away years ago that's only just now looping back around, which
you'd have no idea how to handle because you have no idea what it is.

#details
From *fell*, to cause to fall by delivering a blow + *molechaser*, a low swooping throw of a
boomerang.
::
