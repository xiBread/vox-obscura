::definition
*n.* the wish that people could suspend their civility and indulge the physical side of each other
first&mdash;sniffing each other's hair like dogs, staring unabashedly at interesting faces, reveling
in a beautiful voice like a song on the radio.

#details
Lithuanian *žvėris*, wild beat + Latin *vērissimus*, the truest, the realest. Pronounced
"*zvair*-iz-uhm."
::
