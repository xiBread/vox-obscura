#definition
*n.* a long-overdue argument that shakes up a relationship, burning wildly through your issues like
a forest fire, which clears out your dry and hollow grievances and reminds you that your roots run
deeper than you think.

#details
Middle English *fardel*, burden or bundle + *din*, a loud cacophonous noise. Pronounced
"*fahr*-dl-din."
