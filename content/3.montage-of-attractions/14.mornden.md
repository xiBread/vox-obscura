#definition
*n.* the self-contained pajama universe shared by two people on a long weekend morning, withdrawing
from the world and letting the hours slow to a crawl, coming as close as they'll ever get to pausing
the flow of time, even as they know it'll eventually rush back in all the faster.

#details
From *morn*, morning + *den*, a comfortable room that affords private time. Pronounced
"*mawrn*-duhn."
