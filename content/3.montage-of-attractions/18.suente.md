::definition
*n.* the state of being so familiar with someone that you can be in a room with them without
thinking, without holding anything back, or without having to say a word&mdash;to the extent that
you have to remind yourself that they're a different being entirely, that brushing hair away from
their eyes won't help you see any better.

#details
Southwest English dialect *suent*, easy, peaceful, smooth. Pronounced "soo-*ent*-ey."
::
