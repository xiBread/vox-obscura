#definition
*n.* the disquieting awareness that someone's importance to you and your importance to them may not
necessarily match&mdash;that your best friend might only think of you as a buddy, that someone you
barely know might consider you a mentor, that someone you love unconditionally might have one or two
conditions.

#details
Portuguese *falésia*, cliff. A cliff is a dizzying meeting point between high ground and low ground.
Pronounced "fuh-*lee*-zhuh."
