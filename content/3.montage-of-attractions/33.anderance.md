#definition
*n.* the awareness that your partner perceives the relationship from a totally different angle than
you&mdash;spending years looking at a different face across the table, listening for cues in a
different voice&mdash;an odd reminder that no matter how much you have in common, you're still in
love with different people.

#details
Dutch *ander*, another person, someone else. Pronounced "*an*-der-uhns."
