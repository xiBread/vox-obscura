::definition
*n.* the tranquil pleasure of being near a gathering but not quite in it&mdash;hovering on the
perimeter of a campfire, talking quietly outside a party, resting your eyes in the back seat of a
car listening to friends chatting up front&mdash;feeling blissfully invisible yet still fully
included, safe in the knowledge that everyone is together and everyone is okay, with all the thrill
of being there without the burden of having to be.

#details
Middle English *midding*, alternate spelling of *midden*, a refuse heap that sits near a dwelling.
Pronounced "*mid*-ing."
::
