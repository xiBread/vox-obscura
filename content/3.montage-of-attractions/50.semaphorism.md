#definition
*n.* a conversational hint that you have something personal to say on the subject but don't go any
further&mdash;an emphatic nod, a half-told anecdote, an enigmatic "I know the feeling"&mdash;which
you place into conversations like those little flags that warn diggers of something buried
underground: an unexploded bomb, a sacred burial ground, or a high-voltage cable that secretly
powers your house.

#details
From *semaphore*, a communication method used at sea, in which sailors stand on deck and move flags
into certain positions to convey simple messages. Pronounced "sem-uh-*fawr*-iz-uhm."
