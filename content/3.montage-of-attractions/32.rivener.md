::definition
*n.* a chilling hint of distance that creeps slowly into a relationship&mdash;beginning to notice
them laugh a little less, look away a little more, explain away their like it's no longer your
business&mdash;as if you're watching them fall out of love right in front of you, gradually and
painfully, like a hole in the radiator that leaves your house a little colder with every passing
day, whose only clue is a slow, unnerving *drip*&mdash;*drip*&mdash;*drip*.

#details
Middle English *riven*, to read, to cleave apart. Pronounced "*riv*-uh-ner."
::
