::definition
*n.* the habit of falling hard for whatever pretty new acquaintance happens to come along, spending
hours wallowing in the handful of details you can gather about them, connecting the dots into
elaborate constellations, even imagining an entire future together&mdash;images that have no
particular purpose, except that they're kinda fun to think about.

#details
Hebrew ןירקה (hikrín), to project an image + *hankering*, craving, Pronounced "*hik*-er-ing."
::
