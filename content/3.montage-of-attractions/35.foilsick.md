::definition
*adj.* feeling ashamed after revealing a little too much of yourself to someone&mdash;allowing them
too clear a view of your pettiness, your anger, your cowardice, your childlike
vulnerability&mdash;wishing you could somehow take back the moment, discreetly bolting the door
after a storm had already blown it off its hinges.

#details
Scottish Gaelic *foillsich*, to expose.
::
