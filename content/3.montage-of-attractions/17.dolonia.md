::definition
*n.* a state of unease prompted by people who seem to like you too much, which makes you wonder if
they have you confused with someone else&mdash;someone flawless, selfless, or easy to understand
from a distance&mdash;feeling vaguely disappointed that they're unwilling to spend the time it takes
getting to know the real you.

#details
Ancient Greek εἴδωλον (*eídōlon*), a phantom image of an ideal form. Pronounced "duh-*loh*-nee-uh."
::
