::definition
*n.* a flash of real emotion glimpsed in someone sitting across the room&mdash;their mind wandering
away from whatever's happening around them, their eyes lighting up with pensiveness or vulnerability
or cosmic boredom&mdash;as if you could see backstage through a gap in the curtains, watching actors
in costume mouthing their lines, fragments of bizarre sets waiting for some other production.

#details
From *fata morgana*, a kind of mirage that warps the appearance of distant objects so that sailboats
look like fairy castles + *organa*, methods by which a philosophical investigation may be conducted.
Pronounced "*fah*-tuh awr-*gah*-nuh."
::
