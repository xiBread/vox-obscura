#definition
*n.* a fear you sometimes experience while leaving a loved one, wondering if this will turn out to
be the last time you'll ever see them, and whatever slapdash good-bye you toss their way might have
to serve as your final farewell.

#details
Greek αντίο (*antio*), farewell + -φοβία (-*phobia*), fear. Pronounced "an-tee-uh-*foh*-bee-uh."
