::definition
*n.* the point in your early adolescence when you realize that your parents are muddling through
their lives the same as you; that many respectable adults are no less lost than you and your
friends, no less petty and obsessive and insecure, which makes you wonder if there are no real
adults, because such a thing never actually existed, except in bedtime stories.

#details
German *Kinder*, children. Refers to *Kinder Surprise*, a foil-wrapped chocolate egg that contains a
small toy that's already broken into pieces. Pronounced "thuh *kin*-der ser-*prahyz*."
::
