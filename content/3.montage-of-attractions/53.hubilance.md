#definition
*n.* the quiet poignance of your own responsibility for someone, with a mix of pride and fear and
love and humility&mdash;feeling a baby fall asleep on your chest, or driving at night surrounded by
loved ones fast asleep, who trust you implicity with their lives&mdash;a responsibility that wasn't
talked about or assigned to you, it was assumed to be yours without question.

#details
From *hub*, the central part of the wheel that bears the weight + *jubilance*. Pronounced
"*hyoo*-buh-luhns."
