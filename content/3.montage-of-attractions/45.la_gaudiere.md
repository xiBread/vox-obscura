#definition
*n.* a glint of goodness you notice in someone that you wouldn't expect, which is often only
detectable by sloshing them back and forth in your mind until everything dark and gray and common
falls away, leaving something shining at the bottom of the pan&mdash;a rare element hidden deep in
the bedrock, that must've been washed there by a storm somewhere upstream.

#details
French *la gaudière*, from Latin *gaudere*, to find joy. Pronounced "lah gou-dee-*yair*."
::
