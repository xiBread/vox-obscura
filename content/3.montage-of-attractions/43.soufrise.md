::definition
*n.* the maddening thrill of an ambiguous flirtation, which quivers in tension halfway between
platonic and romantic&mdash;*maybe, but no, but maybe*&mdash;leaving you guessing what's going on
inside their chest, forced to assume that at any given moment their attraction is both alive and
dead at the same time.

#details
French *sourire*, smile + *frisson*, a shiver of chill or excitement. Pronounced "soo-*freez*."
::
