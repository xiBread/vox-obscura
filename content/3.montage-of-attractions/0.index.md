# Montage of Attractions

::quotation
---
author: Louise Erdrich
source: Painted Drum
---

Life will break you. Nobody can protect you from that, and living alone won't either, for solitude
will also break you with its yearning. You have to love. You have to feel, it is the reason you are
here on earth. You are here to risk your heart. You are here to be swallowed up. And when it happens
that you are broken, or betrayed, or left, or hurt, or death brushes near, let yourself sit by an
apple tree and listen to the apples falling all around you in heaps, wasting their sweetness. Tell
yourself that you tasted as many as you could.
::
