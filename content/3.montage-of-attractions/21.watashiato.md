#definition
*n.* curiosity about the impact you've had on the lives of the people you know, wondering which of
your harmless actions or long-forgotten words might have altered the plot of their stories in ways
you'll never get to see.

#details
Japanese 私 (*watashi*), I + 足跡 (*ashiato*), footprint. Pronounced "wah-tah-shee-*ah*-toh."
