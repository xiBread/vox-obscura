#definition
*adj.* longing for a sense of certainty in a relationship; wishing there were some way to know ahead
of time whether this is the person you're going to wake up next to for twenty thousand mornings in a
row, instead of having to count them out one by one, quietly hoping your streak continues.

#details
Mandarin 确认 (*quèrèn), confirmation. Twenty thousand days is roughly fifty-five years. Pronounced
"*kweh*-ruh-nuhs."
