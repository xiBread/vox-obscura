::definition
*n.* feeling wistful open brushing past a person you once shared a life with&mdash;noticing the same
touch on the arm, seeing the same smile, hearing th same laugh you used to adore&mdash;suddenly all
too aware that it's no longer for you, and no longer carries the meaning it once did.

#details
Old English *sithen*, since then + *natheless*, not less by that. Pronounced "*sih*-thuh-les."
::
