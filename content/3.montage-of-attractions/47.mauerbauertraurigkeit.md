::definition
*n.* the inexplicable urge to push people away, even close friends whose company you generally
enjoy&mdash;like a poker player who keeps folding a promising hand in order to avoid the pain of
losing, or tamp down the urge to go all-in.

#details
German *Mauerbauer*, wall-builder + *Traurigkeit*, sadness. Pronounced
"mou-er-bour-er-*trou*-rikh-kahyt."
::
