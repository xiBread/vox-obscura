::definition
*n.* the frustration that you'll never be able to understand another person's pain, only ever
searching their face for some faint evocation of it, then rifling through your own experiences for
some slapdash comparison, wishing you could tell them truthfully, "I know exactly how you feel."

#details
Latin *dolor*, pain + *colorblindness*. Pronounced "*doh*-ler-blahynd-nis."
::
