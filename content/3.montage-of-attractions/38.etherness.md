#definition
*n.* the wishful feeling of looking around a gathering of loved ones, all too aware that even though
the room is filled with warmth and laughter now, it won't always be this way&mdash;that the coming
years will steadily break people away into their own families, or see them pass away one by one,
until there comes a time when you'll look back and try to imagine what it felt like to have everyone
together in the same place.

#details
From *ether*, an intoxicating compound that evaporates very quickly + *togetherness*. Pronounced
"*eth*-er-nis."
