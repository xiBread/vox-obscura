#definition
*n.* the phenomenon of observing your parents interact with people as they grew up with, which
reboots their personalities into youth mode, offering you a glimpse of the dreamers and rascals they
used to be, before you came into the picture.

#details
Refers to Marty McFly, the protagonist of *Back to the Future*, who travels back in time and
interacts with his parents while they're still in their teens.
