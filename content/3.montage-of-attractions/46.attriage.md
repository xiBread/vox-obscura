::definition
*n.* the state of having lost all control over how you feel about someone&mdash;not even trying to
quench the flames anymore, but lighting other fires around your head just hoping to contain the
damage.

#details
From *atria*, the chambers of the heart + *triage*, the sorting of patients in hospital admissions,
factoring in the urgency of their illness or injury. Pronounced "at-ree-*ahzh*."
::
