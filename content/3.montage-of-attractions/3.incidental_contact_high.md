::definition
*n.* an innocuous touch by someone just doing their job&mdash;a barber, yoga instructor, or friendly
waitress&mdash;that you find more meaningful than you'd like to admit; a feeling of connection so
stupefyingly simple it makes you wonder if aspiring novelists would be better off just offering
people a hug.

#details
In sports, *incidental contact* is a glancing touch that doesn't rist to the level of a foul. A
*contact high* is when you feel the effects of a drug vicariously.
::
